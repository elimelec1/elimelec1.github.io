Cute Orange Flowers

  At the bottom of the ocean there are
  orange flowers or
  maybe in your backyard.
  You have to swim really deep to get
  There
  And you might not remember everything.
  This water might be cold blue cold mornings
  Foggy breath,
  Bubbles that giggle and echo
  And you’re tiny!
  Slowly sinking to the bottom of the ocean because people
  forgot how small your hands were
  holding your purple rabbit.
  I couldn’t tell you much more about the orange flowers
  Against the endless sky in which you fell
  Up and grew,
  Sweet and misty pink and
  Faded.
